As the reign of Rev 3 came to a close, Josh, Lee and Chris once again set out to find the final pieces of a new sound.

Dave, formerly of Spectres of Chaos, joined their pantheon in August of 2019 and the final piece of this new creation,

Jim, with his modern take on classic metal vocals, fell into place shortly after.

Together, under a singular, fateful purpose, they comprise Virginia St. Revival.
